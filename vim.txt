This is vim notes

vim . to open file
m to create/edit/add childnode
:q to quick
:wq to save and quit
:/ to search for text in script
ctrl wl to move right
ctrl wh to move left
x to delete text character
dd to delete whole line
2dd to delete two lines
h to move
k to move
l to move 
j to move
gg to move to top of page
v to go into visual mode
